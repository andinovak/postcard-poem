poem: |
  Poppies shine 
  in the morning light
  Delicate petals 
  eager and bright
  In a golden haze
  as daylight fades 
  Poppy petals cup their face 
  in a tender embrace
  Silence falls as the evening stills
  Poppy lanterns light up the fields.
